# I'm a business person, not IT, how do I setup a Vibe Analytics environment against my data?  

Go to chatgpt and enter the following prompt.  

Feel free to change any of it based on your needs.  


```text
I just took an excellent seminar on Vibe Analytics.  Querying data with an LLM and a Jupyter/Spark notebook.  I want to do this against my data at my company but I'm not technical.  Can you give me FIVE IDEAS on how I can get started as simply as possible?  What do I need?  

Here's what I know:  

* I'd like this to be cheap/free
* IDE:  I'd prefer to use vscode.  How would I do that?  
* Data source: I have a read only login to my SQL Server that sits on my local LAN.  I have the connection string.  
* LLM/AI access:  I can sign up for a free github/github copilot account but I don't know how.

```

You'd be surprised at just how helpful chatgpt can be in this case.
